> agriculture economic structure

调整优化农业经济结构，实现农业增效、农民增收是当前和今后相当长时期内农村经济工作的重要任务。
农业发展新阶段的成绩和现存的主要问题说明，发展高效特色农业是新阶段的增长点，把实施特色农业产业化经营作为农业结构调整的基本定位，以特色农业产业化经营带动农业结构的调整，从而实现农村和农业经济的可持续发展。
关键词：农业经济结构 特色农业

To adjust the agriculture economic structure, so as to increase the agriculture benefits and farmers' gain, is an important task in the rural economic work, for the moment and a rather long period.
The achievement in the new agriculture progress phase and the existing problems showing that, the point of growth is to develop a high effcient, particular agriculture.
Set industrializing the particular agriculture as the basic position, base on that, bring on the overall adjustment of agriculture economic structure, thereby, having implemented the continuable progress of rural and agriculture ecnomic.
